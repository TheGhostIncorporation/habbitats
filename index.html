Animal Adaptations & Habitats

Desert Animals

Animals in the desert, like camels and fennec foxes, have evolved to store water efficiently and tolerate extreme temperatures.

Rainforest Animals

Rainforest creatures, such as sloths and poison dart frogs, have unique adaptations to blend in, climb trees, or warn predators with bright colors.

Polar Animals

Polar animals, like penguins and polar bears, have thick fur or blubber to withstand freezing temperatures.

Ocean Animals

Marine life, such as dolphins and jellyfish, are adapted for swimming efficiently and surviving in diverse underwater conditions.
